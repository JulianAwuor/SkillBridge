About Me

I’m a passionate and dedicated sign language instructor committed to empowering individuals through the language of signs. Whether you’re a beginner looking to learn the basics of sign language, or you want to deepen your skills for personal or professional growth, I’m here to guide you every step of the way. My mentorship program is designed to cater to your learning needs, from mastering the alphabet and common phrases to developing fluency in sign language conversations. Let's work together to break communication barriers and open doors to a more inclusive world!

Experiences

Sign Language Mentor & Consultant Freelance | 2021 – Present

I’ve worked with numerous individuals and organizations to improve communication through sign language. With personalized mentorship, I teach beginners to advanced learners the art of sign language, helping them break down communication barriers. I specialize in American Sign Language (ASL), fingerspelling, and basic conversational skills, empowering learners to confidently communicate in various settings.

Sign Language Instructor Inclusive Learning Academy | 2019 – 2021

I led sign language courses for students of all ages, creating an engaging and supportive environment for them to master sign language. I focused on building practical communication skills that students could apply in everyday interactions. My work contributed to increasing accessibility and inclusion in the community, enabling more people to connect through the power of sign language.
